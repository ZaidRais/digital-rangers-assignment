"Shushen"

We derive our name from the royal Vaidhya Sushena from Ramayana. He's popularly known for his greatest gift to mankind - "Sanjeevani Booti" a life-elixir herb. It was Vaidhya Suṣhēna who guided Hanuman Ji to bring Sanjeevani Booti from the Himalayas to save Laxman Ji's life.

Our Story

“For every illness or ailment known to mankind, God has created a plant that will heal it. We just need to keep discovering the properties for natural healing.”

-Dr Anil Agrawal

Shushen Herbals is the brainchild of Dr Anil Agrawal a renowned Ayurveda doctor, who established the brand in 1994 at Vidisha, Madhya Pradesh. Dr Agrawal was very fortunate to born in Vidisha, a small ancient town in Madhya Pradesh which was known as Bes Nagar during 2nd Century BCE.

Dr Anil Agrawal has been successful in formulating more than 200 ayurvedic formulations over a clinical experience of more than 30 years. He personally supervises the production processes of all medicines, syrups, kadhas, capsules, etc. And in his free time he likes to research and write about Ayurveda.

The production facility at Shushen is approved by the WHO-GMP and Ayush Ministry. With an experience of more than 25 years and more than 2 lakh loyal customers nationwide, we are one of the pioneers in the modern-day Ayurveda industry in India.
